What is Pickles Galore?

Pickles Galore is the only restaurant you'll ever need to go to again in your life if you don't want to eat anything but pickles ever again. Our shockingly creative pickle dishes will keep you entertained and overweight for years to come. Luckily you won't have to choose any meals ever again because once you've entered Pickles Galore, you are never allowed to leave. Think of it as the Hotel California of tasty little green bois.
